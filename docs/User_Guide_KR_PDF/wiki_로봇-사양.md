# 로봇 사양 (Robot Specifications)

Source: https://github.com/RobomationLAB/RaccoonBot_Guide_KR/wiki/%EB%A1%9C%EB%B4%87-%EC%82%AC%EC%96%91

## Physical Dimensions

- Size: 93mm × 118mm × 180mm (W × D × H)
- Weight: 825g (excluding gripper)

## Performance

- Working radius: 240° with 295mm reach
- 4 degrees of freedom with 0.0440° joint precision
- Micro-stepping stepper motors with integrated reducers
- BLE 5.0 connectivity for iOS/Android devices

## Battery & Power

- Lithium-ion battery charged via 5V USB-C
- Supports Teaching & Playback (unplugged coding)
- Compatible with RobomationLAB, Scratch, and Python

## Axis Parameters

- Joint 1: ±120°
- Joint 2: -90° to +30°
- Joint 3: -150° to 0°
- Joint 4: ±105°

## Troubleshooting

Guidance for common issues: power-off problems (via reset switch), stopping operations during unplugged mode (using POWER button), and LED diagnostic codes for charging voltage errors, internal communication faults, or data corruption.
