# 추가 장치 (Additional Devices)

Source: https://github.com/RobomationLAB/RaccoonBot_Guide_KR/wiki/%EC%B6%94%EA%B0%80-%EC%9E%A5%EC%B9%98

## Overview

Documents additional equipment for the RaccoonBot robotic arm, covering end-effectors and peripheral devices.

## End-Effector (말단 장치)

"말단장치는 라쿤봇의 가장 끝부분에 장착되어 실제로 물체를 잡거나, 놓거나, 이동시키는 역할을 담당하는 장치입니다" (end-effectors are mounted at the robot's extremity to grasp, release, and move objects). These use a replaceable modular design.

## Finger Gripper (집게 그리퍼)

Standard end-effector features:
- Two-point symmetrical gripping structure
- Modular mounting with 4-pin connector
- Teaching & Playback integration for automated operation recording

## Peripheral Devices (주변 장치)

Expand robot capabilities through modular connections using 4-pin connectors and support Teaching & Playback functionality.

## Conveyor System (컨베이어)

A peripheral device enabling bidirectional control with adjustable speed, linked operation with robot tasks, and automatic state recording during Teaching & Playback modes.

## Control Tables

Includes operational specifications for conveyor button inputs (single press, long press) and LED status indicators showing operational modes in different colors (green, cyan, yellow, orange, purple).
